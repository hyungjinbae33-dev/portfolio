Our Story

We believe in objects that earn their place in your home

FORMA was founded in 2021 with a simple conviction: the objects we live with should be made with integrity, designed with purpose, and built to last.

We travel to studios across Europe, Japan, and the Americas — meeting makers, understanding processes, and selecting only pieces that meet our standards for craft, material, and design.

Every object in our collection has been held, tested, and approved by our team before it reaches yours.
