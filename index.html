About

The Person Behind
the Microphone

With over 15 years capturing sound across the Mediterranean, I bring every scene to life through meticulous audio craft.

Based in Athens, I've worked on productions spanning from intimate documentaries in remote Greek villages to large-scale international feature films. My approach combines technical precision with deep sensitivity to the sonic character of each location.

I believe great location sound is invisible — when done right, it places the audience inside the scene without them ever noticing.
